3 Cheese Omelete

By Jonathan Hill

I make this breakfast when I have some free time to really enjoy a good breakfast.

Ingredients:
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pepper jack cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Colby jack cheese
What to do:
  1. Crack the 3 eggs into bowl and beat until mixed.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and pepper, as well as the 1/2 cup of milk. Mix throughly.
  3. Pour mixture into pan and set temperature to about 5/8ths of the maximum temperature.
  4. Cook until a solid base has formed with the egg.
  5. Flip so that you may cook the other side.
  6. Add a pinch of the pepper jack, colby jack, and cheddar cheese on top of the cooked, flat egg.
  7. After cheese has melted slightly, fold the egg in half to create the omelete.
